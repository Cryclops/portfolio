Activity Claims


Claims for Scenario #1

  • Claim 1: Dave's Library of music.
    • + Dave was able to find a requested song using a Youtube video that he stream into his software.
    • - The sound quality of the Youtube song was worse than the songs in Dave’s library.
  • Claim 2: Dave's equipment.
    • + His new AndroiDJ software is small, portable, and easily handled. He can change songs quickly and efficiently from anywhere in the house.
    • - His phone is relatively small and as he puts his it in his pocket, he changes the frequency of the song and has to quickly fix the issue. Sometimes smaller isn’t the way to go.
  • Claim 3: Software Depth.
    • + His new AndroiDJ software has advanced capabilities, such as pitch shifting.
    • - Dave isn't a full master of his software. When he accidentally changes the song's frequency, he is at first confused. The nested "advanced" capability left Dave feeling confused and panicked.

Claims for Scenario #2

  • Claim 1: The mash-ups create interesting effects for the audience.
    • + Blending two songs creates new, interesting effects.
    • - Finding two songs that blend well with each other is not a trivial matter.
  • Claim 2: The ability to create mash-ups is worth the trouble of setting up the software to do so.
    • + Mash-ups are not heard very often, so DJs should incorporate the ability to create mash-ups.
    • - Learning to use the software may take some time.
