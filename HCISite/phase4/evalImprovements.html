Evaluation: Prototype Improvements


We talked to our client, as well as other designers in our informal Design Studio, and we took their input of our design to improve our prototype. The following list is the changes we made with a description of why we made the change.


Buttons

  • BEFORE: Our buttons were identified by text.
  • AFTER: Our buttons are identified by icons.
  • WHY: Icons are much easier and quicker to read and understand than buttons.
  • VIEW: The new buttons.

Additional Functionality

  • BEFORE: Our system didn't implement the AUTO button or the Pitch button, as well a few other minor things
  • AFTER: Our system now implements all buttons and shows full functionality.
  • WHY: Now we can have a detailed evaluation.
  • VIEW: AutoEQ drawing. AutoEQ options. Playlist view (in process of moving about a song).

Volume Sliders

  • BEFORE: No affordance of which direction more/less volume was in except Up or Down.
  • AFTER: Tapered triangle and color gradients beside sliders.
  • WHY: Clearly delineates which direction is for more volume. Conforms to common design paradigm.
  • VIEW: Volume sliders.
