Claims for Scenario #1

  • Claim 1: Dave's Library of music.
    • + He had 5 hours worth of great music.
    • - He didn't have one of the requested songs, which caused some issues
  • Claim 2: Dave's equipment.
    • + He uses a laptop full of music that easily connects to the speakers.
    • - His laptop and other equipment are stationary in one spot on stage. He has to run back and fix any problem he encounters.
