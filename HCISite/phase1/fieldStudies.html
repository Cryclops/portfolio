Field Studies


In our field studies, we interviewed several types of people. One type was the client-type, a person who actually DJs, and the other was the listener-type, a person who enjoys DJ work and has watched DJs at work. For the clients, we had an extension to the normal set of questions that targeted their enhanced knowledge. The questions are as follows.


  • Listener Type
    • If you have seen a DJ perform, what did you like about the DJ's performance?
    • What are some pros and cons about DJ systems you have seen (at parties, concerts, etc.)?
    • Have you ever been disappointed by the size of a DJ's music library?
    • If you were a DJ, what features would you like to see on your DJ interface?
  • Client Extension
    • What are the pros and cons of your current DJ software?
    • Do you prepare a lot for DJ performances, or do you improvise?
