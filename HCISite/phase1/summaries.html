Summaries


Points for a Cloud-based Library

Overall, the audience is the most important thing when it comes to being a DJ. DJ’s need to be able to connect with the audience through their music and the way they present their music. Having the right features and the right software is the key. The biggest feature both the listener and a DJ want to see is a giant library of music. It seems as if both parties would enjoy DJ-ing more if the DJ had an endless choice of songs. That way, the DJ can avoid repetition and be able to play whatever the listeners want to hear during a performance or performances.


It also seems that a large library is key to beign able to effectively respond to the crowd's demand. If the DJ recieves a request for a song he doesn't have in his library, he is put in an awkward position throughout the show. It sounds like a song has the potential to make or break a night in some situations.


Points for Multiple Tracks

Two of our Client-Types agreed that having more than 2 audio tracks was incredibly useful for creating a dynamic, new/exciting sound. Alex talked about how he created basic mash-ups by combining a bass track, a vocal track, and a melody, along with sound effects.


The need for good sound effects seems pretty essential to creating a good relationship with the crowd. Wes, Alex, and Mike each talk about this. From this, I think we can infer that having some prerecorded effects (such as builds, crashes, and rolls), would be heavily used by our stakeholders


Points for a Deep Feature Set

In talking to Wes, we found out that a DJ needs to have all the necessary equipment to put on a good performance. His software, usually used on a computer, needs to have a variety of features; the most basic being the ability to play two songs at once in order to mix or transition from one to the other. Other features include being able to control the speed of the song (BPM or beats per min) and the ability to control other effects like lights, additional sounds like sirens, and “scratching” each song.
