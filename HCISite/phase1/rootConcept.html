Root Concept

  • High Level Vision: To design a unique touch-based system that will allow DJs to do their job more efficiently, as well as opening up new possibilities; such as touch-based EQ-ing, a cloud-based library, and a new interface design.
  • Basic Rationale:
    • Currently, DJs are using standard laptops. They mainly interact with the software using a mouse or touchpad.
    • DJs are limited by the WIMP (Window, Icon, Menu, Pointing device) design paradigm.
    • A touchscreen would allow for new innovations and happier DJs
  • Stakeholders:
    • Novice DJ - Needs to be able to play two songs at once and control each song's volume.
    • Advanced DJ - Need the ability to have complete control over the music that they are playing. The basic need is the same. Any other attributes that can be controlled are a bonus, and the more attributes a DJ has masterful control over, the higher the skill level of the DJ. They may desire effects, browsers, and automations.
  • Initial Assumptions:
    • DJs are not currently using touchscreens to perform.
    • DJs are supportive of a new interaction paradigm.
    • The current software's interface can be improved upon.
    • The product will have no direct competition.

We arrived at this root concept after thinking about our stakeholders and considering the current situation. We thought about how stakeholders often get frustrated with using laptops, especially if they need to work quickly with the touchpad. Then we thought about current solutions to the problem, which involved 3rd party hardware controllers.


With this in mind, we thought it would be nice to combine the two. Give the DJs a better interface integrated into the computer screen, instead of having to use 3rd party hardware. We realized that combining the two would create a brand new interface paradigm, since the user would receive immediate computer feedback upon changing the display, where as previously they would have to look back and forth between their laptop display and the 3rd party hardware.


Since this will be a new paradigm, we are excited by the new abilities we can offer DJs. We intend to fully explore new innovations in DJ software.
